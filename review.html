Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book travel package?

You can book a travel package directly through our website by navigating to the "Packages" section and selecting your desired destination and dates. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team for personalized assistance.

Where can I find The cancellation policy?

Our detailed cancellation policy can be found on our "Terms and Conditions" page, usually linked in the footer of our website. Specific cancellation terms may also vary depending on the package and will be clearly stated during the booking process.

Is transportation included in the package?

Transportation inclusion varies by package. Some packages include flights, transfers, or local transport, while others may not. Please check the "Inclusions" section of each specific package description for details.

What payment methods are accepted in Kimberly?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For specific details on payment options available for your booking, please refer to the payment section during checkout.
